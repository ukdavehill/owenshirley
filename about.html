I’m an award-winning* Sound Editor, a Sound Designer and a Recording Artist.

I live in Bristol (with an accent to prove it!) and I work freelance for clients in film, TV, commercials and the arts.

My work has featured in TV series, feature films, short films, museum exhibitions, A/V installations and corporate communications.

I’m a member of the BFI/BAFTA Crew, Bristol Crew and BECTU (not to mention Taveners Cricket Club!).

I’m also quite obsessed with bird calls. And I have a metal plate in my arm after losing a fight with a 200 year old footpath.

*and I mean AN award – winning the award for Best Sound, Drama at the Royal Television Society West of England Awards 2018, sharing the award with Andrew Wilson (Re-recording Mixer), Scott Hazell (Composer) and Dave Mackie (Director) for the short film “Breakdown’.
